DecimalPipes and Percent

Pipes included in Angular - Usually in the common module

{{netSales | number:'1.2-2'}}
  • {{netSales + 2000000 }}
  • {{netSales + 2000000 | currency }}
  • {{netSales + 2000000 | currency:'CAD' }}
  • {{netSales + 2000000 | currency:'CAD':'symbol-narrow' }}
  • {{netSales + 2000000 | currency:'CAD':'symbol-narrow':'1.4-4' }}
  • {{percentNumber }}
  • {{percentNumber | percent }}
  • {{percentNumber | percent:'2.2-2' }}
